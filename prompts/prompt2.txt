Example:

Summary: Retrieve all customers who have placed an order in the last 30 days.

SQL Query:

`SELECT DISTINCT c.CustomerID, c.FirstName, c.LastName, c.EmailAddress
FROM Customer c
JOIN SalesOrderHeader soh ON c.CustomerID = soh.CustomerID
WHERE soh.OrderDate >= DATEADD(day, -30, GETDATE());`
